Staff Analytics and Workload Management Feature - M5 Enhanced Features
========================================================================

FEATURE OVERVIEW:
================
A comprehensive staff analytics system that provides detailed insights into staff workload, teaching assignments, time allocation, and performance metrics. This feature enables staff members to better understand their work patterns, optimize their schedules, and compare their performance with departmental benchmarks.

CORE FUNCTIONALITY:
==================

1. WORKLOAD ANALYTICS
   - Working hours per semester with weekly/monthly breakdown
   - Activity-wise time distribution (teaching, administrative, OD processing)
   - Workload trend analysis and alerts for overwork/underutilization
   - Comparison with previous semesters and department averages

2. TEACHING ANALYTICS
   - Subject-wise period allocation across different classes
   - Grade-wise teaching distribution (Grade 1-12, Post-Graduate)
   - Class size analytics and student count tracking
   - Teaching efficiency metrics and load balancing insights

3. TIME ALLOCATION TRACKING
   - Daily/weekly schedule breakdown by activity type
   - Time spent on different responsibilities:
     * Teaching (regular, extra, substitution, lab, practical)
     * OD request processing and approval
     * Administrative tasks and meetings
     * Preparation and evaluation work
     * Other academic activities

4. EFFICIENCY METRICS
   - OD processing performance:
     * Average processing time per request
     * Approval/rejection rates
     * Response time analytics
     * Student satisfaction scores
   - Comparative benchmarking:
     * Department-level comparisons
     * Institution-wide benchmarks
     * Peer performance analysis

5. COMPARATIVE ANALYTICS
   - Semester-over-semester performance comparison
   - Trend analysis for workload and efficiency
   - Performance improvement/decline identification
   - Historical data analysis and pattern recognition

WORKLOAD MANAGEMENT:

DATA MODELS AND STRUCTURE:
=========================

CORE DATA ENTITIES:
- StaffWorkloadData: Comprehensive workload information
- TeachingAnalytics: Subject and class allocation details
- TimeAllocationAnalytics: Activity-wise time distribution
- EfficiencyMetrics: Performance and efficiency indicators
- ComparativeAnalytics: Historical and peer comparison data

SUPPORTING ENTITIES:
- Period: Individual teaching period information
- TimeSlot: Specific time allocation details
- SubjectAllocation: Subject-wise teaching assignments
- ClassAllocation: Class-wise teaching responsibilities
- ActivityType: Categorization of different staff activities

ENUMERATIONS:
- Grade: Academic levels (Grade 1-12, Post-Graduate)
- PeriodType: Types of teaching periods (Regular, Extra, Lab, etc.)
- ClassType: Class categories (Regular, Honors, Remedial, etc.)
- SubjectType: Subject categories (Theory, Practical, Lab, etc.)
- ActivityType: Staff activity categories
